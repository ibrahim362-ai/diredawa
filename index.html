Administrative Structure

Kebeles & Gandas

Dire Dawa City Administration is organized into multiple kebeles covering both urban and rural areas. Each kebele is further divided into gandas (neighborhoods), which serve as the smallest administrative and community units, ensuring effective local governance and community participation.
